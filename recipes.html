Breakfast Recipes

⏱️ 25 Min

Ragi Dosa

High in calcium and fiber, ideal for a nutritious breakfast.

×

Ragi Dosa

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Ragi Flour
  • 1 Onion
  • 1 Green Chilli
  • Salt

Method

  1. Mix all ingredients with water.
  2. Prepare smooth batter.
  3. Pour on hot tawa.
  4. Cook both sides and serve.

Benefit: Rich in Calcium & Fiber.

⏱️ 40 Min

Pongal

Protein-rich traditional pongal that keeps you full longer.

×

Thinai Pongal

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Thinai (Foxtail Millet)
  • 1/4 Cup Moong Dal
  • 1 tsp Black Pepper
  • 1 tsp Cumin Seeds
  • 10 Cashew Nuts
  • 2 tbsp Ghee
  • Salt to Taste
  • 3 Cups Water

Method

  1. Wash Thinai and Moong Dal thoroughly.
  2. Pressure cook with water for 3 whistles.
  3. Heat ghee and roast cashews.
  4. Add pepper and cumin seeds.
  5. Mix with cooked millet and dal.
  6. Add salt and stir well.
  7. Serve hot with coconut chutney.

Benefit: Rich in Protein, Fiber and Easy to Digest.

⏱️ 25 Min

Kambu Koozh

Cooling and energy-boosting traditional millet porridge.

×

Kambu Koozh

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Kambu (Pearl Millet) Flour
  • 3 Cups Water
  • 1 Cup Curd or Buttermilk
  • Salt to Taste
  • 1 Small Onion (Optional)

Method

  1. Mix kambu flour with water without lumps.
  2. Cook on low flame until thick and smooth.
  3. Allow it to cool completely.
  4. Add curd or buttermilk and mix well.
  5. Add salt and chopped onions if desired.
  6. Serve chilled for best taste.

Benefit: Rich in Fiber, Improves Digestion and Keeps the Body Cool.

⏱️ 25 Min

Samai Upma

Light, fiber-rich breakfast for healthy digestion.

×

Samai Upma

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Samai (Little Millet)
  • 1 Onion (Finely Chopped)
  • 1 Green Chilli
  • 1 tsp Mustard Seeds
  • 1 tsp Urad Dal
  • Curry Leaves
  • 2 Cups Water
  • Salt to Taste
  • 1 tbsp Oil

Method

  1. Wash Samai thoroughly and keep aside.
  2. Heat oil and add mustard seeds, urad dal and curry leaves.
  3. Add onions and green chilli, sauté until soft.
  4. Pour water and add salt.
  5. When water boils, add Samai and mix well.
  6. Cook on low flame until water is absorbed.
  7. Serve hot with coconut chutney.

Benefit: High in Fiber, Easy to Digest and Supports Weight Management.
